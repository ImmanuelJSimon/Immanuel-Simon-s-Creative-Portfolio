Butterfly Puzzle Toy

Objective:

The project's primary objective was to conceptualize, design, develop, and construct a butterfly puzzle toy equipped with mechanisms that enable wing flapping. Throughout the course of this undertaking, I gained proficiency in utilizing Solidworks, the chosen application for constructing the butterfly model. Additionally, the project provided a valuable learning experience in 3D printing, enabling me to employ a 3D printer for the actual fabrication of the butterfly toy in real life. This hands-on exploration of Solidworks and 3D printing significantly expanded my skill set and practical knowledge in product development and manufacturing processes.
